Amusement


Amusement is the state of experiencing humorous and entertaining event or in a situation while the person in talk maintains the experience and is associated with emotion such as enjoyment, happiness, laughter and pleasure. It is often an emotion that is positive such as laughter or emotion with high physiological arousal.

Amusement is an emotion when one hears a punchline or joke. People react to the pleasent surprise from learning the new information with a smile, laugh or a chuckle.

Amusement can be detected through facial expression or maybe with a sound of genuine laughter. The laughter will cause peoples muscle to relax

Pleasure

Pleasure is consider as a mental experience that humans have when they find something enjoyable, positive or worth seeking. it can be happiness, entertainment, enjoyment or ecstasy.

Pleasurable experiences usually associated with satisfying basic biological drives such as eat, sleep, hygiene and sex. Other things such as activities, such as art, music, dancing is also consider pleasureable.

Pleasure can also come as an effect of human emotion. people feel pleasure when happy, or when they agree on something, or maybe when they think something is nice or good.
