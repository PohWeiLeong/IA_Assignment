Why is it important to have fun?

have fun, be entertained is a way to walk for a brigther way in life

  • Reduced Stress : Some fun a day keeps people healthy, it helps people laugh and have fun, and thus release the high stress hormones.
  • Better Sleep Quality : having less stress means having less thoughts at nights, it will give people a better quality and sounder sleep.
  • Increased Creativity: Ideas pop out of the mind when people having less stresss, play when needed to get the creativity going.
  • Help Heal Emotional Wounds: having fun lets people create more good and happy memories, over the time some bad memories can be erased.
  • Increase Productivity: reduced unnecessary stress and let the mind run free when it is needed, and energy might increased to avoid burnout.
